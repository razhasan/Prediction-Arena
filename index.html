⚽ How To Play

⚔️ Attacking (you have the ball)

  • Keyboard: Arrow keys to move · A to pass · S to shoot
  • Mouse / Tap on D-pad: Click the on-screen D-pad to move · click Pass or Shoot
  • Mouse cursor: Move your cursor over the pitch — your player follows it · one click = pass · two quick clicks = shoot
  • Touch gesture: Pinch once on the pitch to pass · pinch twice quickly in a row to shoot

🛡 Defending

  • Keyboard: Arrow keys to move · S to tackle
  • Mouse / Tap on D-pad: Click the on-screen D-pad to move · click Tackle
  • Mouse cursor: Move your cursor over the pitch — your defender follows it · click to attempt a tackle
  • Touch gesture: Pinch on the pitch to attempt a tackle

🥅 Penalties

  • Click or tap left, center, or right to aim your shot — or to dive for a save
Use whichever suits your device — keyboard, mouse, and touch all play the same game.
